--------StorageLayer/Micro-partitions/Partition Pruning 
Partition Pruning in Snowflake (Micro-Partition) means Snowflake automatically skips unnecessary micro-partitions and scans only the micro-partitions that may contain the required data.
	If a filter column value exists in all micro-partitions, that column gives no pruning benefit.
	Snowflake depends on other columns' metadata (like EMP_ID) or clustering to reduce scanning.
	Good clustering means similar values are stored together, making pruning more effective.
